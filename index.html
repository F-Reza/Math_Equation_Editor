LaTeX Tips

  • Fractions: \frac{numerator}{denominator}
  • Exponents: x^{2}
  • Subscripts: x_{1}
  • Square root: \sqrt{expression}
  • n-th root: \sqrt[n]{expression}
  • Integrals: \int_{a}^{b} f(x) dx
  • Summation: \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i
  • Matrices: \begin{matrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{matrix}
